Understanding

Subcarpati it's a cultural manifest. Subcarpati it's a project started in 2010 by the performer of the Suie Paparude band, Mc Bean (Alexe Marius Andrei), which combines elements from the electronic music and the hiphop with romanian folklore influences. The music genre is described as contemporary folklore or underground folklore and generaly it consists in interpretations of songs from the romanian foklore on modern beats.

In the project there were involved many more artists like Gnom(Raparta), DJ Limun from Timisoara, Mara and Dragonu from the band Specii , Vj Andri, Dj Power Pe Vinil , Vali Umbra , Argatu', Esqu', Georgiana Malaina from Bucharest and many more.Also, the project is sustained by dozens of bands and artists:AFO, Geneza,Chimie, OCS and not only. The band has been described in the british newspaper The Guardian as an explosive mix between old and new. It brings together an eclectic mix of the melancholy of Romanian folk songs and songs specific unit, traditional instruments and rhythms of trip-hop, dubstep, hip-hop and dancehall.
